About Us

Where Community
Meets the Tiles

Mahjong Social at Solara is a friendly, community-driven studio in Norman, Oklahoma, where players of all experience levels can come together. Whether you're learning the basics or refining your strategy, you'll find a welcoming table and great company waiting for you.
