Our Story

Why We Opened Our Doors

Mahjong Social at Solara was born from a simple idea — that the beauty of this ancient game deserves a welcoming, modern home. We fell in love with mahjong's perfect balance of strategy, luck, and community, and knew Norman needed a place where anyone could learn, laugh, and connect over tiles.

We believe the table is for everyone. Whether you've never touched a tile or you've been playing for years, you'll always find a warm seat here.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to buy anything?

No — we provide everything you need to play. Just bring yourself and we'll take care of the rest!

How many people can you teach at a private lesson?

Private lessons can accommodate groups of varying sizes. Contact us to discuss your group and we'll put together the perfect experience for you.

If I already have a mahjong set, can we use it to learn on during our lesson?

Absolutely! We love when students bring their own sets — it's a great way to get comfortable with the tiles you'll be playing with at home. Just let us know in advance.

Can beginners attend social play events?

Yes! Our social play events are designed to be welcoming at every level. More experienced players love helping newcomers, and it's a wonderful way to learn in a relaxed setting.

Do you offer Corporate events?

Yes! Corporate mahjong events make for a fantastic and memorable team-building activity. Reach out through our contact page to discuss packages, group size, and availability.

I just booked my lesson. What's next?

You'll receive a confirmation email with all the details. Just show up at the scheduled time — we'll handle everything else. We can't wait to play with you!

I need to cancel. What are your policies?

Please contact us at least 48 hours before your scheduled session for a full reschedule or refund. Late cancellations may be subject to a fee — reach out and we'll always do our best to work with you.
