Why We Opened Our Doors

Mahjong Social at Solara was born from a simple idea — that the beauty of this ancient game deserves a welcoming, modern home. We fell in love with Mahjong's perfect balance of strategy, luck, and community, and knew Norman needed a place where anyone could learn and connect over tiles. We believe our tables are for everyone. Whether you've never touched a tile or you've been playing for years, you'll always find a friendly spot at our tables.
